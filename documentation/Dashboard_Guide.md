# Dashboard Guide

This document provides an overview of the Power BI dashboards included in the Hotel Revenue & Guest Experience Analytics project.


# Dashboard 1: Hotel Revenue & Demand Analytics

## Overview

The Hotel Revenue & Demand Analytics dashboard provides insights into booking performance, pricing metrics, customer demand patterns, and cancellation behavior across hotel operations.

The dashboard enables users to analyze booking activity by hotel type, market segment, and customer country while monitoring key revenue-related KPIs.


## Dashboard Filters

### Hotel Type
* City Hotel
* Resort Hotel

### Segment Type
* Aviation
* Complementary
* Corporate
* Direct
* Groups
* Offline TA/TO
* Online TA
* Undefined



## KPIs

### Total Bookings
Total number of reservations analyzed.

**Result:** 119K+



### Average Length of Stay
Average number of nights per booking.

**Result:** 3.43 Nights


### Average Daily Rate (ADR)
Average room revenue earned per occupied room.

**Result:** $101.83



### Cancellation Rate
Rate/Percentage of bookings that were canceled.

**Result:** 0.37 or 37%


### Repeat Guest Rate
Percentage of bookings made by returning guests.

**Result:** 3.19%



## Visualizations

### Average Daily Rate by Hotel Type
Compares ADR performance between City Hotels and Resort Hotels.

**Business Question:**
Which hotel type generates higher room revenue?


### Booking Volume by Month
Displays monthly booking demand patterns.

**Business Question:**
How does booking demand vary throughout the year?


### Top 5 Countries by Booking Volume
Shows the countries generating the highest number of bookings.

**Business Question:**
Which geographic markets contribute the most demand?


### Market Segment Performance
Compares booking volume across acquisition channels and customer segments.

**Business Question:**
Which market segments generate the most bookings?


### Cancellation Rate by Segment
Displays cancellation behavior by market segment.

**Business Question:**
Which customer groups are most likely to cancel reservations?



## Key Findings

* Online Travel Agencies generated the highest booking volume.
* Portugal was the largest source market by bookings.
* City Hotels achieved slightly higher ADR than Resort Hotels.
* Groups and Online TA segments experienced elevated cancellation rates.
* Repeat guest activity remained relatively low.



# Dashboard 2: Guest Experience & Review Analytics

## Overview

The Guest Experience & Review Analytics dashboard evaluates guest satisfaction, review behavior, and customer feedback trends using over 515,000 hotel reviews.

The dashboard provides visibility into sentiment patterns, reviewer demographics, hotel rankings, and review activity trends.



## Dashboard Filters

### Sentiment
* Positive
* Neutral
* Negative

### Review Year
* 2015
* 2016
* 2017

### Hotel Name
Allows users to filter results for individual hotels.



## KPIs

### Total Reviews
Total guest reviews analyzed.

**Result:** 515K+



### Average Review Score
Average guest satisfaction rating.

**Result:** 8.40 / 10


### Average Positive Review Length
Average number of words in positive reviews.

**Result:** 17.78 Words



### Average Negative Review Length
Average number of words in negative reviews.

**Result:** 18.54 Words



### Positive Review Rate
Percentage of reviews classified as positive.

**Result:** 65.09%



### Negative Review Rate
Percentage of reviews classified as negative.

**Result:** 10.18%



## Visualizations

### Guest Sentiment Distribution
Displays the proportion of positive, neutral, and negative reviews.

**Business Question:**
What is the overall sentiment of guest feedback?



### Monthly Review Activity Trends
Tracks review volume throughout the year.

**Business Question:**
How does guest engagement change over time?



### Top 10 Guest Nationalities by Review Volume
Shows which customer groups contribute the most reviews.

**Business Question:**
Which nationalities provide the highest volume of feedback?



### Top 10 Hotels by Guest Rating
Ranks the highest-rated hotels based on average review score.

**Business Question:**
Which hotels deliver the strongest guest experience?



### Bottom 10 Hotels by Guest Rating
Ranks the lowest-rated hotels based on average review score.

**Business Question:**
Which hotels may require operational or service improvements?



## Key Findings

* Average guest satisfaction reached 8.40 out of 10.
* Positive reviews represented over 65% of all reviews.
* Guests from the United Kingdom submitted the highest volume of reviews.
* Significant differences existed between the highest-rated and lowest-rated hotels.
* Most guest feedback was positive, indicating generally strong customer satisfaction.
