# KPI Definitions

This document outlines the key performance indicators (KPIs) used throughout the Hotel Revenue & Guest Experience Analytics project.


# Hotel Revenue & Demand Analytics KPIs

## Total Bookings

The total number of hotel reservations included in the dataset.

**Business Purpose:**
Measures overall demand and booking activity.


## Average Daily Rate (ADR)

The average revenue earned per occupied room.

**Formula:**

ADR = Total Room Revenue ÷ Rooms Sold

**Project Result:**
$101.83

**Business Purpose:**
Evaluates pricing performance and revenue generation.


## Average Length of Stay 

The average number of nights guests stay per booking.

**Formula:**

ALOS = Total Nights Stayed ÷ Total Bookings

**Project Result:**
3.43 Nights

**Business Purpose:**
Helps understand guest behavior and occupancy patterns.



## Cancellation Rate

The percentage of bookings that were canceled.

**Formula:**

Cancellation Rate = Cancelled Bookings ÷ Total Bookings 

**Project Result:**
0.37 

**Business Purpose:**
Measures booking reliability and potential revenue loss.


## Repeat Guest Rate

The percentage of bookings made by returning guests.

**Formula:**

Repeat Guest Rate = Repeat Guests ÷ Total Guests × 100

**Project Result:**
3.19%

**Business Purpose:**
Evaluates customer loyalty and retention.



# Guest Experience & Review Analytics KPIs

## Total Reviews

Total number of guest reviews analyzed.

**Project Result:**
515,000+ Reviews

**Business Purpose:**
Measures customer feedback volume.


## Average Review Score

Average guest rating across all reviews.

**Formula:**

Average Review Score = Total Review Scores ÷ Total Reviews

**Project Result:**
8.40 / 10

**Business Purpose:**
Measures overall guest satisfaction.


## Positive Review Rate

Percentage of reviews classified as positive.

**Formula:**

Positive Review Rate = Positive Reviews ÷ Total Reviews × 100

**Project Result:**
65.09%

**Business Purpose:**
Measures positive customer sentiment.



## Negative Review Rate

Percentage of reviews classified as negative.

**Formula:**

Negative Review Rate = Negative Reviews ÷ Total Reviews × 100

**Project Result:**
10.18%

**Business Purpose:**
Measures negative customer sentiment and service issues.



## Average Positive Review Length

Average number of words contained in positive reviews.

**Project Result:**
17.78 Words

**Business Purpose:**
Provides insight into customer engagement and feedback detail.



## Average Negative Review Length

Average number of words contained in negative reviews.

**Project Result:**
18.54 Words

**Business Purpose:**
Helps understand how extensively guests describe issues and concerns.
